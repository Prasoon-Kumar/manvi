About Us

MANVI PUBLISHERS & DISTRIBUTORS is a well-known Books, Notebooks, and Stationery distributor in Delhi NCR. It has a retail outlet and direct distribution channel for books, Notebooks, and stationery supply to Schools, Institutes & Colleges.

  • Committed to providing superior products and services at the highest level of quality and professionalism
  • Believe in leveraging on the latest publishing technology to deliver quality products and services for the benefit of our customers
  • The Same level of service whether the requirement is for a single book or multiple copies.
  • Also sourcebooks from different publishers and book distributors.
  • Design customized stationery needs as per school requirements
  • Quick, effective and transparent transactions
  • Return facility
  • Packaging of books as per request

MANVI PUBLISHERS & DISTRIBUTORS is a Retailer of high-quality educational resources to meet all curriculum requirements for schools. The product range includes all the top publishers for pre-primary, primary, and secondary levels in English-medium schools. which follow the curriculum such as CBSE, ICSC, etc.

WHO WE ARE

At Manvi Publisher & Distributor , we are not just in the business of publishing books; we are dedicated to nurturing the literary dreams of authors and connecting their words with readers who hunger for meaningful stories. Our journey began with a passion for literature and a vision to bridge the gap between talented writers and avid readers.

  • 01 Q: What more does company bring to the table as a publisher?

    A: Beyond the realm of the printed word, our purpose transcends. We endeavor to cultivate a community of authors who not only inspire and challenge but also kindle contemplation. Our approach to author relationships is nurturing, preserving their creative visions, amplifying their voices, and flourishing through collaboration.

  • 02 Q: How does compnay differentiate itself?

    A: We stand out through an unwavering pledge to quality and innovation. Every story, irrespective of its uniqueness or niche, deserves to be impeccably told. Our team of adept editors, designers, and publishing experts toil assiduously to ensure each book meets the pinnacle of literary finesse and aesthetic allure.

    A: Our commitment extends to distribution as well. As a dynamic distributor, we ensure the reach of our authors' works is far-reaching and diverse. With an extensive distribution network and robust affiliations with bookstores, online retailers, and libraries, we ensure our authors' narratives traverse the globe.

Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the FAQ section of Manvi Publishers & Distributors. Here, we aim to provide answers to your most common questions about our services, processes, and how we can collaborate to bring exceptional literary works to readers around the world.

  • What is the focus of Manvi Publishers & Distributors?

    Manvi Publishers & Distributors is dedicated to fostering a thriving literary ecosystem. We operate as both a publishing house and a distribution platform, connecting authors, retailers, and readers to create a seamless journey for literary content.
